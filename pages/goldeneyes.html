Vancouver Goldeneyes

Professional Women Hockey League (PWHL)

About

The Vancouver Goldeneyes are a professional ice hockey team competing in the Professional Women's Hockey League (PWHL), playing their home games at the Pacific Coliseum. The team's identity is inspired by the Common Goldeneye, a bird native to Vancouver's waterways, with colors of Pacific Blue, Coastal Cream, and Earthy Bronze.

History

The Goldeneyes are the PWHL's first announced expansion franchise, joining the league for the 2025-26 season alongside the Seattle Torrent. The team name was unveiled on November 6, 2025, and they opened their inaugural season on November 21 against Seattle at the Pacific Coliseum. They are the first PWHL team to be named after an animal. The 2025-26 season is the only season in franchise history so far.
